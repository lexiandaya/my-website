Friendly Animals

Unlike the hostile mobs, the animals will not hurt you at all. These are the animals that will roam around your world on a daily Minecraft basis. They can also be a great source for food...

Sheep

When you generate a new world, there is a high chance you'll see a sheep right away. Some of their natural spawn colors are white, light and dark grey, black, and brown. If you happen to run into a pink sheep, feel lucky because they are rare! To breed two sheeps together to make a baby sheep, you'll need just two pieces of wheat. As long as you hold it in your hand, they will follow you.

Cow

Similar to sheep, Minecraft cows are quite common throughout your gameplay experience. They will also follow you if they see you are holding wheat, and can also be bred with it. Additionally, when you craft a bucket and right click a cow with it, the bucket will be filled with the cow's milk.

Pig

Pigs are great to have around. Unlike the sheep and cow, pigs will only drop raw porkchop when slain. However, that doesn't make them useless in terms of materials. Put a saddle on one, craft yourself a carrot on a stick, and you have yourself a new ride. The durabilty of the carrot on a stick doesn't last very long, so enjoy it while you can.
