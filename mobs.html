Hostile Creatures

Here are some of the monsters you will encounter while playing. They spawn at night and in the dark, so it is important you have sufficient light source!

Zombie

The zombies are the least intimidating monster you will come across. Besides their creepy growls and snarls, they aren't the strongest and are the easiest to defeat. A Zombie villager is one variant of a regular zombie. They can be pretty common amongst abandon villages if you ever stumble upon one while exploring.

Skeleton

These monsters can be such a pain in the butt. Skeletons carry a bow and arrow making it difficult to get a hand on them from a close range. Although their shooting accuracy can be pretty sharp, it isn't impossible to get withing hitting range. On rare occasions, you may see a skeleton riding a spider. That is a Spider jockey. They are still able to shoot and cause damage, but the spider is nothing but its ride.

Creeper

One monster you want to avoid getting close to, creepers have the ability to explode like TNT. WIthout proper protection or insufficient health, there is a chance you will need to respawn. If do happen to trigger a creeper, there is a chance the items in your inventory will not survive, either.





When slain, there is almost a 100% chance zombies will drop some rotten flesh. In other occasions, there is a chance they will drop carrots or potatoes.











While skeletons can be difficult to defeat, they will usually drop bones and arrows. Sometimes you will get lucky and obtain their bow! However, its durabilty will not last you very long.









If you manage to terminate a creeper without having it blow up, these guys will drop gunpowder. Just like in real life, you can use gunpowder to craft TNT for whatever you desire.
